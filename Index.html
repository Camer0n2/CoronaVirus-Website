Corona Virus

By Cameron Shortt

The Corona Virus is a international pandemic that is taking the lives of many people around the world, with reported cases all over the globe.

The Corona Virus, also known as COVID-19 is the most recently discoverd virus in the corona viruses family. The corona viruses family can cause illneses to humans and animals.

There are many things that you can do to prevent yourself from catching and spreading corona virus to you friends and family. some of these ways include wearing a face mask and washing your hands for 20 seconds all over.
